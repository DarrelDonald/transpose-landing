**Phase 02 ‚Äî Form Security:**
1. **Input validation** ‚Äî All user inputs should have reasonable `maxlength` attributes to prevent abuse:
   - Email: `maxlength="254"` (RFC 5321 limit)

   ```
   Content-Security-Policy: default-src 'self'; script-src 'self'; style-src 'self' 'unsafe-inline' https://fonts.googleapis.com; font-src 'self' https://fonts.gstatic.com; img-src 'self' data:; connect-src 'self' https://formspree.io; frame-ancestors 'none';
   ```
   
2. **X-Frame-Options** ‚Äî Prevent clickjacking: `X-Frame-Options: DENY`
